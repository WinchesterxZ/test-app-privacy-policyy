Deletion of Account and Personal Data

You have the right to delete your account and associated personal data from our system at any time. To do so, you can either:

Use the "Delete Account" option available in the app settings.
Contact us directly to request account deletion.
Once you request deletion, all your personal data, including your name, email address, and any other data associated with your account, will be permanently removed from our databases.

Please note that once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
