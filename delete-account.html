Account Deletion Instructions for Foodify

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from the Foodify app, follow the instructions below:

Steps to Remove Foodify from Your Facebook Account:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on Settings.
  3. Navigate to Apps and Websites, where you will find a list of all your app activities.
  4. Locate the option for "Foodify".
  5. Click the "Remove" button next to Foodify.
