Privacy Policy for Foodify

Effective Date: February 2, 2025

Foodify is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect
We collect the following personal data:

Name
Email address
We collect this data when you sign up using Firebase Authentication, Facebook, or Google login. We do not collect or use any sensitive personal information, such as payment details, location data, or health information.

Usage of Your Information
We use your personal data to:

Provide authentication for accessing the app.
Personalize your experience within the app.
Send notifications for app-related updates and improvements.
Store some images on Firebase Storage.
Third-Party Services
We use third-party services for the following purposes:

Firebase for user authentication, storage, and notifications.
Facebook and Google for sign-in purposes.
We do not share your information with any other third parties, except as required by law.
Data Retention
We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the services and as required by law. If you choose to delete your account, all associated personal information will be permanently deleted from our databases.

Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
Correction: You can update or correct your personal data.
Deletion: You can delete your account and associated personal data by contacting us directly or by using the "Delete Account" option in the app settings. Once you delete your account, all associated personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.
Data Security
We employ encryption to ensure the safety and confidentiality of your data. All personal data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you through the app or other means, and the updated policy will be posted on this page.
